# Project Brief — Deloitte V&I Team Dashboard

## Context
Deloitte team members navigate a fragmented landscape of internal tools — timesheets, compliance training, resourcing, payroll, expenses, and more — each living at a different URL, often discovered only through tribal knowledge. This dashboard provides a single, curated entry point to eliminate that friction.

**Current stage:** Website prototype  
**Initiated by:** V&I (Ventures & Innovation) design team

---

## Problem
- No single place to find all the tools you need day-to-day
- New joiners rely on "insider intel" from teammates to discover systems
- Context-switching between unrelated tools creates cognitive overhead

---

## Audience
**Primary:** Designers in the Deloitte V&I team  
**Future:** All teams at Deloitte Digital

Assume users are digitally literate but time-poor. They know what they need to do; they just need to find it fast.

---

## Tone
- **Clear over clever** — label things what they are, not what Deloitte calls them
- **Calm and organised** — not a busy intranet portal
- **Practical** — utility first, personality second

---

## Constraints
- Prototype only — no backend, no auth, no live data
- Links must open in a new tab (external Deloitte tools)
- No design system guidance in this document (provided separately)
- Must work for teams beyond V&I without major rework
- Cannot store or transmit any credentials or sensitive data
