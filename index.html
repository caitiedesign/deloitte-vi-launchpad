Your first-week checklist — complete these in order.

  1. 1
    Set up Cirrus — confirm bank details, check leave balance, download the app. Open Cirrus →
  2. 2
    Complete mandatory MyQ&R modules — due within your first 30 days. Open MyQ&R →
  3. 3
    Submit your first timesheet — log hours by end of week. Ask your manager for project codes. Open Timesheets →
  4. 4
    Check Float — see which projects you're rostered on and who your teammates are. Open Float →
  5. 5
    Complete your Independence declaration — required within your first two weeks. Open Independence Check →
  6. 6
    Join the V&I Teams channel — announcements, retros, and social stuff. Open Teams →
